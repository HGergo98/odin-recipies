Fruit soup

Strange though it may seem, in Hungary fruit soup is eaten as a soup, not as a dessert.
It's best to make the soup with various fruits e.g. cherry, sour cherry, apple, plum or strawberry.

Ingridients:

  • 3 apples, peeled and diced
  • 3 handfuls of sour cherries
  • pinch of salt
  • 1-2 tbsp sugar
  • 2-3 whole cloves
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
  • zest of a half lemon
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 200 ml (~3/4 cup) whipping cream
  • 1 egg yolk

Steps:

  1. Place the fruits in a pot, pour in water, just enough to cover them.
  2. Add cloves, lemon zest, vanilla, salt and sugar, bring it to a gentle boil, and simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. In a small bowl whisk together cornstarch and a half cup of water. No lumps should remain in the mixture.
  4. Pour it into the soup and stir. Cook for further 1 minute. Meanwhile whisk together the whipping cream and the egg yolk.
  5. Ladle some hot liquid to the mixture and whisk together.
  6. 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
  7. Turn off the heat, and, while stirring continuously, in a fine stream pour the yolk-cream mixture into the soup (don't cook or the egg will scramble).
  8. Let it cool a bit, and serve warm or let it fully cool down and serve cold.
